# Disclaimer

Creative Accounting Scanner is a structured document-review aid. It is not investment advice, audit advice, accounting advice, tax advice, legal advice, valuation advice, or a regulatory-compliance opinion.

The skill is not a fraud detector. It does not accuse any company, officer, employee, auditor, underwriter, lender, investor, or other party of wrongdoing.

Red flags require corroboration. A flagged pattern may reflect seasonality, business-model change, acquisition effects, contract timing, macroeconomic pressure, new accounting standards, presentation changes, or other valid explanations.

Public disclosures may be incomplete, unaudited, summarized, stale, or difficult to compare across periods. Users must verify all calculations independently and consult qualified professionals before relying on any conclusion.

Outputs should be read as questions for further review, not as findings of improper conduct.
